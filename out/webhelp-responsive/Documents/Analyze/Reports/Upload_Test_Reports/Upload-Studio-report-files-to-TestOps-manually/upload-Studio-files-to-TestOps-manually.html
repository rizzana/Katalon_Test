Upload JUnit and Katalon Studio report files to Katalon TestOps manually

You can manually upload JUnit reports and Katalon Studio test results to Katalon TestOps.

Follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to Katalon TestOps.
  2. Select the Project you want to store the reports.
  3. Go to Reports > Test Runs.

  4. Click on the Upload Reports button in the top right corner.

    • You can either upload JUnit or Katalon Studio reports.

  5. Click Choose Files and browse the files in your local machine.

    For JUnit reports, select the .xml file.

  6. Optionally, you can assign custom fields and tags to your test report by navigating to Custom Fields and Tags sections. Select from the dropdown list below each section the custom fields and tags for your reports. To understand about custom fields and tags, how to create and manage them, see Custom Fields and Tags.

  7. Click Import.

You have uploaded the reports successfully.
